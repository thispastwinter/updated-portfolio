About Me

I'm currently a student of KU's Coding Bootcamp and when I'm not in class, I'm helping open up a bar/distillery under the name of Mean Mule Distilling Co. We are Kansas City's first agave spirit! It's been an absolute blast to be a part of something that's being built from the ground up, but there is a lot of stress in balancing everything.

I'm looking forward to the challenge and hope this will be a great opportunity to learn to manage my time efficiently. In addtion to taking this class and managing a bar, I'm also a musician. I've been recording music since high school and regularly play live. This year my long time collaborator/best friend and I plan to release a full length album. It will be the first "new wave" album either of us have worked on. It's been a fun challenge exploring a new genre.
